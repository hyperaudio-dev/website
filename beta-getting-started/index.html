Beta - Getting Started

We'd love you to test out our service for remixing transcribed media. It's early days, so you're going to find a few bugs, but don't fret, just let us know about them and we'll find a way to iron them out.

There are many individual parts to the Hyperaud.io ecosystem, for now we think the Hyperaudio Pad and the Hyperaudio Converter are ready for testing.

The Hyperaudio Maker that auto-transcribes media will come in v0.5 and the Hyperaudio Cleaner in v0.6 (watch out for a roadmap document soon).

Here's a quick intro to the Hyperaudio Pad.

And here's a suggested list of things you can try...

1. Sign up and sign in

First, head over to the signup page and sign up. For now you will be prompted to login, later we'll do the whole email confirmation fandango.

2. Check Out some Mixes

Click on the mixes you see once you log in to see what it's possible to create. At the moment all our media is hosted on YouTube, but this will change. We're also aware that depending on bandwidth, mixes may not work particularly well first time, we need to work on our caching.

3. Make your own Mixes

Once you've seen what's possible, you can click the 'create mix' button on 'MIXES' to make your own remix. Press the save button in the top right corner to save. Your mix should then show up under 'MIXES'.

4. Add Your Own Media

To add a video of your own, click on 'MEDIA' in the top or bottom navigation and click 'Add Media'. Select a YouTube video that has subtitles (TIP You can go to amara.org to find these) and paste the YouTube link into the 'video link' text box, click 'Add Video'. You should see your video appear under 'Your Media'

5. Add a Transcript

Click on the video you added under 'Your Media' Add a 'Channel' name and 'Tags' if you like and click the 'Convert Transcript' button. Now you can paste your (SRT formatted) subtitles into the left hand pane, tweak a few parameters as required and click the 'Transform' button to see the results. Clicking on 'Rendered View' will show you the text as it will be displayed in the Hyperaudio Pad or any applications you make with the transcript. When you are happy with the results click the tick button in the top right corner to save the transcript.

6. Now Mix Your Own Media

You can now remix your transcribed media by navigating to 'MIXES' and clicking the 'Create Mix' button. (Ignore the save warning). Your video should appear under 'Your Media'.

7. Adding and Deleting

You can add / delete transcripts at any time by clicking on your media in 'MEDIA'.

8. Have Fun, Feed Back and Join In

It's early days still, the service is very rough around the edges and much of the material is roughly aligned from subtitles, but we're already excited by the potential of Hyperaud.io. We hope you are too.

Feel free to join the community discussions on our Google Group and follow us on Twitter to keep up with the news.
